Abstract

This project mainly aims at analysing the sentiment of customer reviews on various e-commerce websites. This will help the user to get a rough feedback on the product that they are buying. This allows them to gain an understanding of attitudes, opinions & emotions expressed within an online mention.
Recurrent Neural Networks will be used to perform sentiment analysis for effective product recommendation. This will give the user an insight about how the other buyers feel about the product.
